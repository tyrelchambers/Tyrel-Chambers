Conflict2Profit

Site: www.conflict2profit.com

Published: September 12th, 2015

This was my very first project. After having been fiddling with web development technologies for the better part of a year, I got brought onto a team, or into a startup company I should say, with the role of making a website! I used very basic methods and I learned a lot; loved every bit of it.

This project was a real stretch. Having just broken onto the scene with web technologies, I had a pretty big task ahead of me. Combining patience, time, effort and everything other cliche thing, I produced this website. It was the first time I had used a preprocessor (SCSS) and workflow automation (gulp). This is my first published site and I know it will get better from here.
