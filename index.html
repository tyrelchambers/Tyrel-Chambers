Oh where to begin?

So here is a little about me: I was born on July 9th, 1992 with a passion for computers and video games. As of the last couple of years, I have dove head first into Front-End Development. It all started when I was asked to help my dad with a project for someone from my church. This person like to cook and they do it well! They had a website that needed a little T.L.C (a makeover); so I said "sure, I'll help". I spent a month or so fiddling away at the website using mainly HTML, CSS and PHP (this was the ignition for my passion).

The project ended up falling through due to circumstances outside of our control, but nevertheless, an impression was made. After that, I continued to fiddle, learning new things, teaching myself new languages. My next big personal project was my church's website. It took me a couple months to get it done and I thought it wasn't bad. Looking back now, it was so amateur! I would consider that project there, the catalyst to my growing passion for this field. I continued to create projects for myself, over and over, just because I love what I do!

That was all about two and a half to three years ago and I haven't looked back since. Right now, I am teaching myself better coding techniques and better design practices. As for languages, I have taken an interest in the MEAN stack which stands for MongoDB, ExpressJs, AngularJs, and NodeJs; it's all JavaScript!

All my waking moments, if I'm not working my other job, is spent making projects, streaming on twitch, chatting and helping other developers in the DevTips community, or reading up on new techniques/languages. I love what I do, I love what I know, I love and am excited for what the future holds for me and those I get to help!

With that said, welcome to my site, and feel free to stay a while! P.S. I love landscape/nature photos!
