Gesher Hachaim

The Story

Gesher Hachaim was my first paid piece. I did it on the squarespace platform (not a sponsor...) just because it was easy to create and for the client the maintain in the future. IT gave me enough customization options to allow me to do what I wanted.

I feel this project was one that put everything into perspective. Going from knowing nothing to going through two years of learning and grinding, I finally brought on my first paying client. It's a great feeling and a huge confidence booster!
